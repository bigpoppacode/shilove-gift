About

Rashida, known as Shi Love, is a visionary fashion designer elevating Atlanta's style scene with her bold, culturally-rooted designs. As the creative force behind Shi Love Clothing, she crafts pieces that blend elegance, edge, and authenticity — fashion that makes a statement.

With roots in both the UK and Ghana 🇬🇧🇬🇭, Shi Love's designs reflect a global perspective while staying grounded in Black excellence and cultural pride. Her clothing isn't mass-produced — it's curated, intentional, and designed to make you feel powerful.

Based in Atlanta, Shi Love has built a brand that speaks to those who refuse to blend in. From custom pieces to signature collections, her work has made her one of Atlanta's most sought-after designers.
